No magic, just science...
        and hard work!



We take a very, very thorough approach to cleaning and decontaminating your car's paintwork -
which means that the finish will feel glossy-smooth. Compare this with the usual method of simply
using a coat of very shiny polish right over the top of the contaminants, and you can see why
Amazing Glaze gets the results that we do!

    The process that we follow varies to a degree, according to the state of each individual vehicle, but generally will include the following steps:

  • Paintwork Cleaning

    Five way paintwork clean:
    Shampoo
    Power wash (inc wheel arches)
    Tar and bug removal
    Iron Particle removal
    Claying

  • Paintwork Polishing

    Machine and hand polishing
    Two-layer sealing

  • Wheel Treatment

    Shampoo
    Decontaminate
    Tar removal
    Polishing
    Wheel sealer
    Tyre Treatment

  • Exterior Trim

    Clean
    Conditioner

  • Windows

    Clean
    Hydrophilic sealer

  • Interior

    Vacuum throughout
    Clean mats
    Clean trim
    Clean windows
